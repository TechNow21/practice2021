Example 2

Lab 1 - Part B

Brief Instructions:

Removing Unwanted Object(s) of an Image

In this part of the project, the idea was to remove unwanted object or objects from the picture to make it look different and to the desired look. In the picture, I removed some of the background flowers at the bottom.

I chose to remove some of the flowers in the background because I wanted to know how to revome some items without destroying the way the picture looks.

What I did:

1. Opened image in Photoshop.

2. Used patch tool to remove two background flowers at the bottom of image.

3. Used spot healing brush tool to take out some remaining background light flowers and leaves.

4. Used sharpen tool to sharpen the bird on the branch.

5. Used Image > Image Size to reduce image to 53% original size, resulting image is 1000 pixels x 666 pixels.

6. Exported image for web friendly version.
